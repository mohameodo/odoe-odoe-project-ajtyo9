About Nature Trails

Nature Trails is an interactive mapping project dedicated to inspiring exploration and appreciation of the world's natural wonders. Our goal is to provide a beautiful, easy-to-use tool for discovering famous national parks, planning scenic routes, and connecting with the great outdoors.

Our Mission

To leverage technology to foster a deeper connection between people and nature. We believe that by making natural landmarks more accessible and engaging, we can encourage conservation efforts and promote sustainable tourism for generations to come.

Technology Used

This application is a demonstration of modern web development using fundamental technologies, built without any heavy frameworks.

  • HTML5 & CSS3: For structure and custom styling.
  • TailwindCSS: For a utility-first, responsive design system.
  • Vanilla JavaScript: For all interactivity and application logic.
  • Leaflet.js: An open-source library for interactive maps.
  • Font Awesome: For beautiful, scalable icons.
